General

My name is Cassandra Culkins, and I am currently a student at Epicodus. I have a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Portland State University. The second reason that I am attending is because I have grown up around computers and people who were interested in similar fields, and although I did not see myself in this field before, I have recently come to understand my own interest in it. I have a taste for work that involves creativity and problem-solving and programming certainly has both of those aspects.
